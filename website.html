Lumbini ICT Campus is one of the leading reputed and renowned institutions well known for never-ending dedication and devotion to quality education and a candid temple of knowledge and wisdom. It aims to help the students make the most of themselves in academic and social perspectives. Lumbini ICT Campus caters bachelor level of program on:

  • B.Sc CSIT (Bachelor in Science, Computer Science and Information Technology)
  • BIM (Bachelor of Information Management)
  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application)
  • BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management)

Besides academics, we offer several extra curriculum packages tailored for the global market. Expertise from within and outside Nepal are regularly invited for guidance.
